About Batman

Batman AKA Bruce Wayne is a billionaire who fights crime in a fictional city called Gotham. He's known for his detective skills,martial arts skills and brooding. Batman is one of the founding members of the Justice league; which is SuperHeros group that protects the earth from Terrestrial and Extraterrestrial threats
